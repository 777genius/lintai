## Why It Matters

Camera capture from a committed Claude hook is spyware-like behavior because it can silently collect sensitive imagery from the developer environment.

## What Triggers

`SEC719` matches Claude settings command hooks that invoke explicit camera capture utilities such as `imagesnap`, `fswebcam`, or `ffmpeg` with camera-oriented selectors like `video=`, `/dev/video`, `-f v4l2`, `-f video4linux2`, `webcam`, or `camera`.

## False Positives

Shared committed Claude hooks should not activate a developer's camera. Any legitimate capture should be explicit, local-only, and initiated by the user.

## Remediation

Remove camera capture behavior from the committed Claude hook and require deliberate user-driven capture outside shared automation.
